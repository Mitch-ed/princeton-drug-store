RxLocal Portal

Mobile App

Manage Your Prescriptions from Anywhere

The Princeton Drug Store patient portal, powered by RxLocal, is designed to make managing your medications as easy as possible. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you can request refills, check prescription status, and stay organized — all without making a phone call or waiting in line.

The portal works in any web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer and connects directly to your Princeton Drug Store account. Setup takes just a few minutes, and once you're in, everything is in one convenient place.

Access the RxLocal Portal
Features

Everything You Need, Built In

The Princeton Drug Store patient portal is straightforward and designed with patients in mind — not complexity.

One-Tap Refills

Request a refill on any of your active prescriptions with a single tap. We'll notify you when it's ready for pickup.

Prescription History

View all of your current and past prescriptions in one organized list. Great for keeping track of what you take and when.

Refill Reminders

Set automatic reminders so you never run out of a medication. The app can alert you a few days before you're due to run low.

Pickup Notifications

Get notified as soon as your prescription is filled and ready. No more guessing — you'll know the moment it's good to go.

Family Account Management

Manage prescriptions for your entire family from a single app account. Ideal for parents managing medications for children.

Hours & Contact

View Princeton Drug Store's hours, contact info, and location directly in the app. Call or email us with one tap.

Getting Started

Up and Running in Minutes

Getting set up with the Princeton Drug Store patient portal is quick and easy. You don't need any technical knowledge — just a web browser and a few minutes.

1

Visit the Portal

Go to the RxLocal patient portal using the link on this page, or visit patient.rxlocal.com directly from any browser.

2

Create Your Account

Register with your name, date of birth, and the phone number on file at Princeton Drug Store. Your prescriptions will sync automatically.

3

Manage Your Medications

Request refills, set reminders, and view your prescription history — all from the portal. It's that simple.

4

Pick Up In Store

We'll send a notification when your prescription is ready. Swing by during store hours and you're all set.
